**God of Investing** is an immersive **desktop-simulation (OS Simulation)** game where you step into the shoes of a professional stock broker. Navigate through a virtual operating system to track corporate messages, analyze breaking news, and manage complex portfolios to conquer the financial world.

## üïπÔ∏è About the Game

In God of Investing, your entire world is your computer screen. As a broker at a top-tier firm, you must filter through a constant stream of information. Whether it's a leaked internal memo or a global news headline, every piece of data is a tool for your success. 

While waiting for the markets to react, you can kill time with built-in mini-games or manage your workstation just like a real OS.

### Key Features
- **Authentic OS Experience:** Fully functional virtual desktop featuring window management (minimize, maximize, close), Taskbar, and Alt-Tab functionality.
- **Investment Terminal:** A dynamic stock market system influenced by real-time in-game events.
- **Integrated Apps:**
  - **Investment App:** Monitor your portfolio and execute trades.
  - **News Feed & Messages:** Critical sources of information that dictate market volatility.
  - **Mini-Games:** Play classics like Snake and Flappy Bird to earn extra points and currency.
  - **Media Player:** A built-in music player to set the mood for your trading sessions.

## üöÄ Gameplay Loop

1. **Analyze:** Read incoming messages and monitor the news feed for market signals.
2. **Invest:** Rebalance your portfolio based on your analysis of upcoming trends.
3. **Engage:** While waiting for trade results, play mini-games to boost your earnings.
4. **Ascend:** Increase your capital and reputation to truly become the *God of Investing*.
